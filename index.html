We are Virar Hub

The Virar-Hub Incubator is a pioneering initiative based in Virar, Maharashtra, dedicated to fostering innovation and entrepreneurial spirit in small towns and regions. Founded by renowned tech entrepreneur, Nishant Patel of Contentstack, the incubator provides a robust platform for early-stage startups, offering them mentorship, resources, and strategic partnerships. By tapping into the local talent of the Vasai-Virar region, Virar-Hub Incubator envisions a future where regional innovations drive global change and place the area on the global startup map.

Our Visionary

Promoted by Nishant Patel, the serial tech entrepreneur and Cofounder of Contentstack, a leading composable digital experience platform, VirarHub intends to provide a first of its kind platform for building a culture of entrepreneurship in the VasaiVirar area.

The Team

Joining hands with Nishant in this venture are Rajdip Gupta, Founder and CEO of Route Mobile Ltd., and Kshitij Thakur, a Member of the Legislative Assembly representing the Nalasopara constituency in the Maharashtra State Assembly. While Kshitij is a devoted mentor and supporter of startups in the Vasai-Virar region, Rajdip, a seasoned entrepreneur himself, has pledged his support to this movement.
